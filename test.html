Our Services

SELF SERVICE

The purpose of our self service platform is to empower patients and healthcare providers to take control of their needs. By utilizing our online features, you can streamline your appointment booking process,gain insights into your symptoms, and access clinical tools to diagnose and treat medical conditions efficiently.

HOW TO USE

APPOINTMENTS:

Booking an appointment has never been easier! To schedule an appointment with one of our healthcare providers simply follow these steps.

  • Go to the Home Page and click on the “Book An Appointment” button.
  • Select the type of service you require
  • Choose a date and time that works best for you.

That’s it! Our system would automatically schedule your appointment and send you a confirmation email. if you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, simply log back into your account and make the necessary changes.

SYMPTOMS CHECKER:

If you are experiencing symptoms but are unsure what they could mean, our symptom checker can help. To use this feature simply follow these steps.

  • Go to the Home Page and click on the “Symptom Checker” button.
  • Answer a series of questions about your symptoms
  • Based on your responses, the symptom checker will provide you with a list of possible conditions.

This feature is not intended to replace your doctor and diagnose medical conditions, but it can help you determine whether you should seek medical attention or not.

DDx & MEDICAL PLANNING:

Our website also provides a range of tools for healthcare professionals. One of our most popular features is our DDx and Clinical planning tool, which can help doctors diagnose and treat their patients more efficiently. Here is how to use this feature.

  • Log in to your staff account on our website.
  • Enter your patients symptoms and medical history.
  • The system will provide you with a list of possible diagnosis and treatment options.

This feature is designed to save doctors time and help them make more informed decisions when treating their patients.
